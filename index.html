Biography

My name is Mark Sroufe Jr, and I am from Cincinnati, OH. I have an AAS from Cincinnati State with a focus on Audio/Video Production Technology. While I started with a particular interest in videography and editing, I realized that my passion laid within the stories of the people, products, and events that I've captured and repackaged. This led me to explore the rest of my creative skills and as a result have worked on projects of various scale and type. From creating baby shower invitations, to equipping start-ups with essential branding materials, to creating social media videos to be used as employee recruitment. This has all led me to a new venture which includes building a strong foundation in web development and coding. I'm currently working towards a certicate in coding for full stack development with MERN through MiT xPro.
